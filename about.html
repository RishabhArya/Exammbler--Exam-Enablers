About Our Event!

Event Description


  The participants would be required to register along with selecting a coding
language from the few listed coding languages which they are most comfortable.
    The event would be in two rounds.

Round 1:

There will be an online quiz on the chosen language. 50% of the top participants in the first round would qualify for the second round.


Event Rules


For Round 1 :- 


                             Rule 1 : There will be 30 MCQs with one correct answer.
Rule 2 : The test will be of 30 minutes.
  Rule 3 : This test is an individual round.
